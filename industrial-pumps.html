CENTRIFUGAL NON METALLIC GLANDLESS SEALLESS VERTICAL UHMWPE PUMP

Sealless and glandless vertical pump in Special Grade UHMWPE. Runs dry without damage, perfect for scrubbers.

Centrifugal Non Metallic Glandless Sealless Vertical UHMWPE Pump

Constructed in Special Grade Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene. Fully sealless and glandless vertical structure to prevent fluid leakage at the shaft entry.

Working Principle

The liquid that tries to pass where the drive shaft enters the back plate is diverted and returned as a controlled flow to the suction tank. The leakage is allowed to escape through the overflow connection of the Overflow Chamber. The auxiliary expeller impeller prevents leakage from ascending further, completely eliminating the need for dynamic shaft seals.

Key Features
  • Glandless/Sealless design: Completely eliminates dynamic seals and glands.
  • Dry Run Capability: Can run dry indefinitely without damages.
  • Slurry Handling: Excellent performance with abrasive particles.
  • No discharge restriction damage: Partially or fully closed discharge valves cause no damage.
Applications
  • Scrubbers and gas washing plants.
  • Filter presses and effluent transfer.
  • Ejector systems.
  • Phosphoric acid and caustic soda plants.
