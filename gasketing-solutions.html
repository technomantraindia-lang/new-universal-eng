Spiral Wound Gaskets

Spiral wound metallic gaskets are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature sealing. Champion officially catalogs these main types:

Type G Spiral Wound Gasket

Gasket Only – Without Inner & Outer Ring. Suitable for groove flanges, tongue-and-groove joints, male-female joints, valves, and high-pressure pumps.

Type G Spiral Wound Gasket (Gasket Only)

Design: Spiral wound metallic sealing element only, constructed without inner or centering rings.

Suitability: Best for tongue-and-groove, male-female, and groove flanges. Ideal where the sealing width is restricted.

Specifications

  • Available Materials: Carbon Steel, SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, SS 321, SS 347, Monel 400, Inconel, and Alloy 20.
  • Fillers: PTFE, Expanded Graphite.
  • Applications: Valves, high-pressure pumps, tongue-and-groove flange joints, space-restricted flanges.

Type CG Spiral Wound Gasket

Gasket with Centering Ring. Standard spiral wound gasket with a sealing element and solid metallic outer ring for compression control and centering.

Type CG Spiral Wound Gasket (Centering Ring)

Design: Sealing element combined with a solid metallic centering (outer) ring.

Suitability: Standard ASME B16.20 gaskets for raised-face and flat-face flanges. The outer ring centers the gasket on flange bolts, acts as a compression stop, and prevents blow-out.

Specifications

  • Outer Ring Materials: Carbon Steel (painted/plated), Stainless Steel 304, 316.
  • Winding Materials: Carbon Steel, SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, SS 321, SS 347, Monel, Inconel.
  • Fillers: Graphite, PTFE.
  • Applications: Standard pipelines, raised face flanges, process industries, utility lines.

Type GI Spiral Wound Gasket

Gasket with Inner Ring Only. Designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Prevents turbulence and protects the sealing element.

Type GI Spiral Wound Gasket (Inner Ring Only)

Design: Sealing element combined with a solid metallic inner ring.

Suitability: Suitable for pressure vessels, boilers, manholes, caps, covers, and groove flange applications. The inner ring prevents turbulent fluid flow and protects the winding from process media.

Specifications

  • Inner Ring Materials: SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, SS 321, SS 347, Monel, Inconel.
  • Winding Materials: SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, Monel, Inconel, Alloy 20.
  • Fillers: Graphite, PTFE.
  • Applications: High pressure boilers, vessel manholes, vacuum systems, pumps, and groove flanges.

Type CGI Spiral Wound Gasket

Gasket with Centering Ring & Inner Ring. A heavy-duty gasket designed for high pressure, high temperature, vacuum service, and critical toxic or corrosive media.

Type CGI Spiral Wound Gasket (Centering & Inner Rings)

Design: Heavy-duty design featuring a sealing element, solid metallic outer centering ring, and solid metallic inner ring.

Suitability: The ultimate sealing solution for high-pressure, high-temperature, vacuum service, corrosive/toxic process media, and critical pipeline nodes. Inner ring protects from heat and corrosion; outer ring centers and acts as a compression stop.

Specifications

  • Materials: Carbon Steel (outer ring), SS 316L/304/321/347/Monel/Inconel/Alloy 20 (inner ring & winding).
  • Fillers: Expanded Graphite / virgin PTFE.
  • Standards: ASME B16.20, EN, JIS, DIN, PN.
  • Applications: Hydrocarbon lines, refineries, critical chemical vessels, reactors, and high pressure piping.
