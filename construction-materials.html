TMT BARS

Thermo-mechanically treated steel reinforcement bars designed to provide high tensile strength and earthquake resistance for RCC construction.

TMT Bars (Thermo-Mechanically Treated)

TMT Bars are thermo-mechanically treated steel reinforcement bars widely used in RCC construction. They are designed to provide high tensile strength, better bonding with concrete, and excellent flexibility during construction. Due to their ribbed surface, TMT bars grip concrete strongly and improve the overall strength of structures.

These bars are suitable for residential buildings, commercial projects, bridges, industrial foundations, slabs, columns, beams, and other heavy-duty construction work. Their high ductility also helps structures perform better during vibration and seismic movement.

Key Benefits
  • High tensile strength for strong RCC work.
  • Better bonding with concrete due to ribbed design.
  • Good bendability and flexibility on site.
  • Suitable for earthquake-resistant construction.
  • Corrosion-resistant surface for longer life.
  • Ideal for foundations, beams, slabs, and columns.
Applications

RCC buildings, bridges, columns, beams, slabs, foundations, industrial construction, infrastructure projects.

STRUCTURAL STEEL

High-durability profiles including angles, channels, beams, flats, and hollow tubes engineered for building frameworks and industrial sheds.

Structural Steel Sections

Structural Steel includes steel sections such as angles, channels, beams, flats, square tubes, rectangular tubes, round pipes, and other fabrication materials. It is mainly used where high load-bearing strength, durability, and structural stability are required.

It is widely used in industrial sheds, warehouses, building frames, machinery support structures, platforms, staircases, roofing structures, and fabrication work. Structural steel is easy to cut, weld, drill, and fabricate, making it suitable for custom construction and engineering projects.

Key Benefits
  • Strong load-bearing capacity.
  • Suitable for heavy fabrication work.
  • Easy to weld, cut, and install.
  • Long-lasting and durable material.
  • Available in multiple shapes and sizes.
  • Ideal for industrial and commercial structures.
Applications

Industrial sheds, warehouses, building frames, fabrication work, machinery bases, support structures, platforms, roofing frames.

CEMENT

Premium binding agent used in concrete mix, brick masonry, wall plastering, flooring, and structural RCC foundations.

Construction Cement

Cement is one of the most essential construction materials used as a binding agent in concrete, mortar, plaster, and masonry work. It provides strength, durability, and stability to construction structures. When mixed with sand, aggregates, and water, cement forms concrete that is used in major structural applications.

Cement is used in almost every stage of construction, including foundations, RCC work, brick masonry, plastering, flooring, tiling, and finishing. Good quality cement ensures better setting, strong bonding, smooth finish, and long-lasting construction performance.

Key Benefits
  • Provides strong bonding and structural strength.
  • Suitable for concrete, mortar, and plastering.
  • Ensures durability and stability in construction.
  • Gives smooth finishing for walls and floors.
  • Compatible with sand, aggregate, and admixtures.
  • Used in residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
Applications

Concrete work, RCC construction, brickwork, plastering, flooring, foundations, slabs, columns, beams, masonry work.

PAINTS & PROTECTIVE COATINGS

Decorates and shields interior/exterior walls, metal structures, and wood surfaces from humidity, sunlight, and weathering.

Paints & Surface Coatings

Paints are used to protect, decorate, and enhance the appearance of interior and exterior surfaces. They provide a smooth finish, attractive look, and protective layer against dust, moisture, stains, weather, and surface damage. Paints are available for walls, metal, wood, industrial surfaces, and exterior structures.

Interior paints improve the beauty of rooms and provide washable, smooth, and elegant wall finishes. Exterior paints protect buildings from sunlight, rain, moisture, and pollution. Industrial and metal paints help prevent rust and corrosion on steel and machinery surfaces.

Key Benefits
  • Enhances interior and exterior appearance.
  • Protects surfaces from moisture and weather.
  • Provides smooth, clean, and durable finish.
  • Available in multiple colors and finishes.
  • Helps prevent rust on metal surfaces.
  • Suitable for walls, wood, metal, and industrial use.
Applications

Interior walls, exterior walls, metal surfaces, wooden surfaces, industrial coatings, commercial buildings, residential painting.

ROOFING SHEETS

Durable covering panels in color-coated, corrugated, and galvanized steel designs for warehouses, factories, and sheds.

Roofing & Cladding Sheets

Roofing Sheets are durable covering materials used to protect buildings, warehouses, factories, sheds, and open structures from rain, sunlight, heat, wind, and weather conditions. They are available in different materials such as metal roofing sheets, color-coated sheets, galvanized sheets, and corrugated sheets.

These sheets are lightweight, easy to install, and suitable for long-span roofing applications. They are commonly used in industrial sheds, commercial roofing, parking areas, farmhouses, warehouses, workshops, and residential roofing projects. Their strong profile design helps improve load-bearing capacity and water drainage.

Key Benefits
  • Strong protection against rain, heat, and weather.
  • Lightweight and easy to install.
  • Available in color-coated and galvanized options.
  • Suitable for industrial and residential roofing.
  • Good durability and long service life.
  • Provides quick and economical roofing solution.
Applications

Industrial sheds, warehouses, factories, parking areas, farmhouses, workshops, commercial roofing, residential roofing, storage sheds.
